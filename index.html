Natural Resources

Estimated Reserves

Venezuela has the world's largest proven oil reserves (~303 billion barrels). Currently, production is much lower due to lack of investment and infrastructure. It also possesses immense reserves of Gold, Gas, and Iron.

Sources:
  • U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)
  • U.S. Geological Survey (USGS)

Historical Production & GDP

Historically, Venezuela's GDP closely tracked its oil production and export values. The sharp decline post-2014 reflects falling production levels, dropping from over 3 million barrels per day to under 1 million.

Sources:
  • World Bank Open Data
  • OPEC Annual Statistical Bulletin

Key Insights & Recommendations

Key Findings

  • Venezuela possesses unparalleled raw resource wealth, heavily skewed towards hydrocarbons.
  • Historical data shows extreme vulnerability to single-commodity price shocks (Dutch Disease).
  • Comparisons with Chile and Norway indicate that strong institutional frameworks are more critical than the resources themselves.

Investor Recommendations

  • Prioritize public-private partnerships (PPPs) with strict international arbitration clauses.
  • Diversify portfolio focus towards renewable energy potential (solar/hydro) and eco-tourism.
  • Factor in significant initial CapEx for infrastructure rehabilitation (power grids, ports).
